Roast chicken with veg

A perfect chicken meal that requires almost no clean up, what a treat!

Prep time: 10 mins | Cook time: 45 mins | Serves: 5
Ease: 5/5 | Healthiness: 4/5 | Ease: 4/5 | Price: 3/5

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2-4 chopped sweet potato
  • Spices for the roast
  • Olive oil
  • 1 bag Pick n Pay Roast Vegetables Packet
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • Sauce for flavouring

Instructions

  1. Set the oven to 180℃
  2. Chop up all the vegetables
  3. Place the chicken and vegetables onto the baking tray and place into the oven for 50-60 minutes
  4. Combine the meal with the rice, and add any sauces if needed

Recipe notes

  • I found that flipping the chicken over at around 40-50 minutes allows the bottom to cook a bit better. This is also a great time to mix the vegetables up to get them crispy on all sides.
